

## Racing Car Hero Section

### Design
Inspired by the Formance layout — bold, large typography on the left with a striking visual on the right/background. Adapted for a racing car theme:

- **Video Background**: The provided Cloudinary video URL plays fullscreen behind the hero, with a dark overlay for readability
- **Color Scheme**: Deep black/dark backgrounds with red accents (red gradient overlays, red accent dots, red CTA buttons)
- **Typography**: Bold, oversized heading (using Inter or similar clean sans-serif) — something like "Speed. Power. Precision." or similar racing-themed copy
- **Layout**: 
  - Top nav bar with logo + "Contact Us" button (pill-shaped, like the reference)
  - Large hero text on the left side
  - Subtitle text describing the racing experience
  - A red CTA button ("Explore" or "Discover")
  - Bottom floating nav bar (pill-shaped, like the reference) with links: Home, Cars, About, Contact
  - "SCROLL" indicator at bottom left with a red accent line
